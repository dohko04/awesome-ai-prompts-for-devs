# AI Team Transition Planner

> Companies like Atlassian (1,600 layoffs, March 2026) are restructuring entire engineering orgs around AI. Use this prompt to plan your team's AI transition — whether you're a manager navigating the shift or a developer future-proofing your skills.

## The Prompt

```
You are an engineering leadership advisor specializing in AI-driven team transitions.

Context: [DESCRIBE YOUR TEAM — size, tech stack, current AI usage, industry]

Create a 90-day AI transition plan:

## Phase 1: Audit (Week 1-2)
- Which tasks in our workflow could AI handle today?
- Which roles become MORE valuable with AI (not less)?
- What's our current AI literacy level? (survey template)
- Identify the 3 highest-ROI automation opportunities

## Phase 2: Upskill (Week 3-6)
- Learning paths for each role (engineer, PM, QA, design)
- Recommended tools to introduce first (lowest friction)
- Internal "AI champion" program structure
- Metrics: how to measure productivity gains honestly

## Phase 3: Integrate (Week 7-12)
- New workflow designs with AI embedded
- Which tools to adopt vs build
- Updated job descriptions and hiring criteria
- Success metrics and review cadence

For each recommendation:
- 🟢 Low risk (try today)
- 🟡 Medium risk (needs planning)
- 🔴 High risk (needs leadership buy-in)

Be honest about what AI can't do yet. Don't oversell.
Focus on augmentation over replacement.
```

## When to Use
- Your company is planning an AI-driven restructuring
- You're an engineering manager building an AI adoption roadmap
- You're a developer wanting to understand where the industry is heading
- Post-layoff: rebuilding a leaner, AI-augmented team
